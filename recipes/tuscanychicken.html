Creamy Tuscany Chicken

Description

Juicy chicken in a creamy garlic tomato sauce. Incredibly easy to assemble, and delicious when served with garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Heavy cream
  • Spinach
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken breasts
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook chicken
  2. Melt butter and saute garlic and tomato. Add spinach when tomatos are beginning to burst
  3. Stir in cream and parmesan, bring to a simmer
  4. Return chicken to pan
