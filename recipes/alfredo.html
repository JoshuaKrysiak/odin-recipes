Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccinne alfredo is a simple timeless recipe involving few ingredients. Butter, heavy cream, parmesan cheese, italian seasoning, salt and pepper create a decadent sauce. When mixed with quality fettuccinne, a delicious meal is born.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fettuccinne noodles

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodles until al dente
  2. Melt butter in pan and saute garlic until fragrant
  3. Add heavy cream and bring to simmer
  4. Add parmesan and stir to melt evenly
  5. Add seasonings
  6. Combine sauce with noodles and a bit of pasta water
