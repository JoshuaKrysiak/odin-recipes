Garlic Bread

Description

Crispy golden brown delicious garlic bread. Perfect when paired with soups and creamy dishes.

Ingredients

  • French bread loaf
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Melt butter and add minced garlic
  2. Cut bread in half lengthwise and spread on butter mixture
  3. Sprinkle italion seasoning on top
  4. Broil on high until crispy on top
